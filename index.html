Welcome PixelPlayr!

Parallel Territories by PixelPlay is a 2-in-1 pixel-style browser game.

Portal Runner:
- Auto-runner, jump with Space or tap.
- Land on platforms, use portals for bonuses or teleport.
- Don't fall!

Lock-On Legends:
- Click/tap targets to score.
- Miss 3 times and it's game over.
- Difficulty increases over time.

Use the floating menu in-game for Pause, Restart, Music, and Home.
